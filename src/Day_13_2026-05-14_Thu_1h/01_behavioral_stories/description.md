# Behavioral questions prep

- **Time budget**: ~20 min

## Objective
Have 3-5 polished STAR stories ready. Pull from your 10 years of .NET experience — that's a strength, not a liability.

## Task
In `stories.md`, write out STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) responses for:

1. A time you had to learn something new quickly.
2. A time you handled a production incident.
3. A time you disagreed with a teammate on a technical decision.
4. A time you had to deal with ambiguous requirements.
5. Your motivation for moving from .NET into ML/AI Engineering.

Aim for 90 seconds per story when spoken aloud. Read each one out loud at least once.

## Expected output
5 stories written + practiced once.

## Self-check
Does each story end with a measurable result (latency reduced X%, defects dropped Y%)?
