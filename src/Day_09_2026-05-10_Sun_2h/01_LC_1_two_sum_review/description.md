# LC 1 — Two Sum (REVIEW)

- **Source**: https://leetcode.com/problems/two-sum/
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Time budget**: ~10 min

## Objective
Re-solve from scratch WITHOUT looking at your previous solution.

## Task
Given `nums` and `target`, return indices of the two numbers that add up to `target`.

Solve in one pass with a hash map. Set a 5-minute timer.

## Expected output
Working solution + you can verbalize the approach in <30 seconds.

## Self-check
If you got stuck, what specifically tripped you up? Note it.
